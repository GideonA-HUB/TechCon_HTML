Our History

TechCon began in 2010 as a small developer meetup and has since grown into one of the most influential technology conferences in the world. Each year, thousands of tech enthusiasts gather to share knowledge and innovations.

Our Mission

At TechCon, our mission is to empower individuals and organizations by fostering a collaborative environment for sharing the latest in technology, engineering, and innovation. We aim to drive progress and inclusion in the tech industry worldwide.
